Our Story

Founded in 1978, Sri Manjunatha Saw Mills began as a small family-owned operation in Bhadravathi with a single saw and a commitment to quality. What started as a modest local business has grown into one of the region's most trusted suppliers of timber and wood products.

Over the past two decades, we've invested in modern equipment and trained our team in the latest wood processing techniques, while never losing sight of the traditional values that made us successful: honesty, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve contractors, furniture makers, and homeowners throughout Karnataka, providing them with premium quality wood products for all their needs.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality wood products while maintaining sustainable practices that protect our forests for future generations. We aim to be the most reliable timber supplier in Karnataka through exceptional service and consistent quality.

Our Vision

To become the preferred partner for all wood product needs in Southern India by 2030, recognized for our quality standards, environmental responsibility, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality craftsmanship, environmental stewardship, honest business practices, and building lasting relationships with our customers and community. We believe in growing our business the right way.
