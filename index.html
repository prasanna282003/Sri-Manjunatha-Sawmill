About Our Saw Mill

Founded in 1978, Sri Manjunatha Saw Mills has grown from a small family operation to one of Bhadravathi's most respected wood suppliers. Our commitment to quality and customer service has been the foundation of our success.

We source our wood from sustainable forests and use modern processing techniques to ensure the highest quality products for our customers.
